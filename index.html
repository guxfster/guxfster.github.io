I'm an Associate Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Computing and Mathematics (formerly Department of Mathematics) at University of West Georgia. I joined UWG as an Assistant Professor in August 2015. Previously, I spent two years as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at University of Wisconsin-Superior and one year as a lecturer in the Department of Mathematics at Texas State University. I got my PhD in Mathematics from West Virginia University under the supervision of Dr. Hong-Jian Lai.
